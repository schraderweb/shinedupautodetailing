    About ShinedUp Auto Detailing



    At Shined Up Auto Detailing, we’re more than just car cleaners—we’re car care specialists who take pride in restoring, protecting, and enhancing the vehicles of Traverse City and the surrounding areas. Whether it’s your daily driver, weekend cruiser, or family SUV, we treat every vehicle like it’s our own, delivering exceptional quality and unmatched attention to detail.

    Driven by Passion, Backed by Experience

    Founded by a lifelong auto enthusiast, Shined Up Auto Detailing was built on a love for clean, flawless vehicles and the satisfaction of turning a weathered car into something that looks—and feels—like new. Our mission from day one has been simple: provide premium detailing services with honesty, integrity, and pride.

    We bring years of experience in professional detailing, paint correction, and ceramic coating, using only top-tier products and proven techniques that deliver long-lasting results. Our shop isn't just a business—it's a reflection of our commitment to excellence in auto care.

    Premium Services, Personalized for You

    We know no two vehicles—and no two customers—are exactly alike. That’s why we offer customized detailing packages tailored to your needs, schedule, and goals. From express exterior washes to full interior and exterior restoration, we handle it all with the same level of precision and professionalism.

    Our core services include:

    • Interior Detailing: Deep-cleaning carpets, seats, dashboards, vents, and more
    • Exterior Detailing: Hand washing, clay bar treatments, waxing, and polishing
    • Ceramic Coating: Industry-leading protection for your vehicle’s paint and finish

    Each service is performed with expert-level care, using safe, eco-friendly products that protect both your car and the environment.

    Serving Traverse City and Beyond

    Located right here in Traverse City, Michigan, we’re proud to serve local residents as well as seasonal visitors and car lovers across northern Michigan. Whether you’re preparing for a long road trip, selling your vehicle, or simply want to protect your investment, Shined Up Auto Detailing is your trusted local choice.

    We believe in doing things the right way—no shortcuts, no up-sells, just honest work and high-end results. That’s what keeps our customers coming back and referring us to friends and family.

    Your Satisfaction Is Our Guarantee

    We stand behind every detail. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we won’t let a vehicle leave our shop until it meets our exacting standards—and yours. We’re not just here to clean your car. We’re here to build long-term relationships with clients who care about their vehicles as much as we do.

    Let’s Get Your Vehicle Shined Up

    Thank you for considering Shined Up Auto Detailing. We’re honored to be part of your vehicle’s care and protection journey. Whether you drive a commuter car, luxury ride, or rugged truck, we’re ready to restore its beauty and extend its life—one detail at a time.

    Contact us today or book your detail online and experience the difference of detailing done right.
