    Maintenance Tips to Extend Canopy Life

     

    Boat lift canopies are built tough, but a little care goes a long way in preserving their performance and looks.

     

    Maintenance Checklist:

     
         
    • Clean with mild soap and water every 4-6 weeks
      •    
    • Use UV protectant spray to extend fabric life
      •    
    • Check for loose tension straps or hardware
      •    
    • Remove the canopy in winter if not in use
      •    
    • Store in a dry, rolled-up position (never folded)
      •
